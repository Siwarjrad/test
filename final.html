Our Mission

انا معاك is a Tunisia based company whose mission is to provide the best transportation and companionship services for the visually impaired of all ages، guaranteeing utmost security and comfort. The company will establish its presence in the industry by acquiring partnerships with already existing transportation services and approaching potential investors. Ena Maak will provide complete taxi cab services along with the companionship feature using the necessary resources and technology to facilitate the travel of individuals. The company’s services show that we are a problem solving, forward thinking company that acknowledges our costumers’ needs and recognizes their agony when it comes to everyday transportation journey. Our philosophy is definitely making our customers, our number one priority and provide them with the best service there is, to enhance their experience. The company also emphasizes that effective communication is a key component in our business, and to ensure that along with the safety of our customers, we are committed to provide our employees with the best trainings. All our cars will be clean, well maintained, and inspected regularly for safety and comfort.

Our vision

The company’s long-term goal is to create equal opportunities to special needs people in order to support and sustain an inclusive economic and social eco-system. Ena Maak plans to respond to the ever-changing market needs and consider all disabilities. Within the next few years, we are willing to implement other services and equip our cars with the necessary comfort tools, such as wheelchairs. We are also planning to take the service to other regions outside the capital and expand our map.
